Allignment

Company: Bio Age Labs

Visit Website

Left allignment is always the best option with typography because that is how our brains are programmed to look for information in our society. We always read from left to right, so I think the use of that allignment helps the reader understad where to find the next line of information instead of having to scan the whole page to find what you are looking for.
