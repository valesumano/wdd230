Bountiful Foods was founded in 2005 by Jane and Tom Smith, a husband and wife team who shared a passion for fresh, healthy food. They started as a small farmers market stand in Carlsbad, California, selling locally grown fruits and vegetables to the community.


As demand for their produce grew, they expanded their offerings to include organic options and began sourcing from national and international suppliers to provide customers with a wider variety of fresh produce. Bountiful Foods quickly became known for its high-quality, healthy food options, and the business continued to grow.


In 2010, Bountiful Foods opened its first storefront in Carlsbad, offering customers a convenient location to purchase their fresh produce. The storefront was a hit with the community, and soon after, Bountiful Foods began expanding its reach by adding food trucks to its fleet. These trucks would travel up and down the California coast, serving fresh and healthy food options to tourists and locals alike.


Today, Bountiful Foods has several food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego, as well as a thriving storefront in Carlsbad. Customers can order online or over the phone, and Bountiful Foods offers delivery services for added convenience.
